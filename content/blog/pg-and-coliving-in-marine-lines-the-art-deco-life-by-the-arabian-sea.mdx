### **2. The 2026 Transit Advantage: The "Coastal-Aqua" Synergie**

In 2026, Marine Lines is one of the best-connected localities in Mumbai, benefiting from a "triple-threat" of rail, road, and underground transit.

- **Mumbai Metro Line 3 (Aqua Line):** The **Kalbadevi Metro Station**, located right on the edge of Marine Lines, is fully operational. It connects residents to **BKC** in 25 minutes and the **International Airport** in under 40 minutes, completely bypassing South Mumbai traffic.
- **The Coastal Road (Phase 2):** The Marine Drive-Worli stretch has been expanded, allowing for signal-free movement to the Western Suburbs. What used to be a 45-minute crawl to Bandra is now a breezy 15-minute coastal drive.
- **Western Railway Lifeline:** The Marine Lines station remains a crucial hub, providing direct access to the western corridor, from **Charni Road** to **Borivali**.
- **Atal Setu (MTHL) Access:** Via the Sewri-Worli elevated connector, Marine Lines residents can now access the MTHL in under 15 minutes, opening up fast travel to **Navi Mumbai** and **Pune**.
