Situated at the junction of **Bannerghatta Road and Outer Ring Road**, this location is already one of the **busiest and most commercially active zones in the city**. With metro connectivity now strengthening further, Jayadeva Hospital is rapidly emerging as a **top choice for PG and coliving accommodations**, especially for **students, medical professionals, and IT employees**.
