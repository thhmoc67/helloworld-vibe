## **1. What exactly is coliving?**

Coliving is a professionally managed shared housing model where residents have private rooms and share common areas like kitchens, lounges, and coworking spaces. Unlike traditional PG accommodation, coliving spaces are purpose-designed with quality furnishings, professional management, all-inclusive pricing, and deliberate community programming. HelloWorld operates 250+ coliving spaces across 16+ Indian cities.

## **2. How is coliving different from a paying guest (PG) accommodation?**

The key differences are management quality, physical environment, services, and community. Traditional PGs are typically run by individual landlords with variable quality standards, informal management, and no community programming. Coliving spaces like HelloWorld's are professionally managed to consistent standards, fully furnished, all-inclusively priced, and deliberately designed to build community among residents.

## **3. Is coliving more expensive than a PG or apartment?**

When you compare true all-in costs, coliving is typically more cost-effective than an independent apartment and comparable to or only slightly more than a well-managed PG. HelloWorld's all-inclusive pricing covers rent, utilities, WiFi, and housekeeping  there are no separate bills or hidden charges. A traditional apartment's true all-in cost (rent plus utilities plus furnishing amortised plus maintenance) is typically significantly higher than a coliving room's all-inclusive rate.
