MG Road is the historic and commercial heart of Bangalore. As one of the busiest stretches in the city, it connects business districts, retail corridors, and entertainment zones into a single, high-density urban cluster. The presence of the MG Road Metro Station on the Purple Line has further strengthened its role as a central transit and employment hub.

For professionals relocating to Bangalore—especially those working in central business districts, startup ecosystems, or nearby IT corridors—finding PG or coliving accommodation near MG Road Metro Station is often driven by the need for **centrality, connectivity, and lifestyle access**. This guide provides a detailed breakdown of the residential options, micro-markets, rental patterns, and the role of managed coliving in this area.

## **MG Road in Bangalore’s Urban Structure**

MG Road sits at the core of Bangalore’s central business district, connecting key areas such as **Brigade Road, Residency Road, Church Street, and Cubbon Road**. It is one of the oldest commercial zones in the city and continues to house offices, retail establishments, and hospitality infrastructure.

The MG Road Metro Station functions as a critical node on the Purple Line, linking the central district to:

- **Indiranagar and Whitefield** toward the east
- **Majestic (Kempegowda interchange)** toward the west
- Interchange access to the Green Line via Majestic

This makes MG Road one of the most accessible locations in Bangalore for both work and daily movement.

## **Residential Catchment Areas Around MG Road**

Unlike suburban areas, MG Road itself has limited residential inventory. Most accommodation demand is absorbed by nearby neighbourhoods that offer a balance of proximity and livability.

### **Ashok Nagar**

Located immediately adjacent to MG Road, Ashok Nagar is one of the closest residential zones. It offers premium housing options and is within walking or short commuting distance of the metro station.

### **Richmond Town**

A well-established residential locality with a mix of apartments and independent houses. Richmond Town provides relatively quieter surroundings compared to MG Road while maintaining proximity.

### **Ulsoor**

Situated along the eastern side, Ulsoor offers a combination of residential properties and access to the metro line. It is also connected to Indiranagar, making it a flexible location.

### **Shivajinagar**

A dense and older part of Bangalore, Shivajinagar provides more affordable accommodation options compared to MG Road’s immediate surroundings. It is well connected via road and public transport.

## **Rental Trends Near MG Road**

MG Road is a premium micro-market, and rental pricing reflects its central location.

### **Monthly Rent Estimates**

- Shared PG accommodation: ₹9,000 – ₹14,000
- Private PG rooms: ₹14,000 – ₹20,000
- Shared coliving rooms: ₹12,000 – ₹18,000
- Private coliving rooms: ₹18,000 – ₹30,000

Independent apartments in this zone are significantly more expensive, often starting above ₹30,000 for a 1BHK.

### **Cost Perspective**

The key advantage of coliving in this area is **cost predictability**. Traditional rentals involve multiple additional expenses such as maintenance, utilities, and furnishing, which can significantly increase the total cost.
