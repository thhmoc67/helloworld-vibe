## **1. The Strategic Interchange: Blue and Violet Lines**

The Mandi House Metro Station is a critical underground node that perfectly balances the heritage of Central Delhi with the speed of the 2026 transit network.

- **The Blue Line Spine:** Direct, 2-minute access to **Rajiv Chowk (CP)** and a 30-minute straight shot to the business districts of **Noida** and **Vaishali**. It serves as the primary route for media professionals working at **ITO** or **Film City**.
- **The Violet Line (The Heritage Corridor):** Connects you northwards to **Kashmere Gate** and southwards to the upscale **Khan Market**, **Lajpat Nagar**, and the business hubs of **Faridabad**.
- **The 2026 Connectivity Bonus:** With the new interchanges at **Lajpat Nagar** (connecting to the Golden Line) and **Janpath**, Mandi House residents can now reach the **IGI Airport** and **South Delhi’s tech parks** in under 35 minutes.
- **Exit Gates for Art Lovers:** The station features four strategic gates. **Gate No. 3 and 4** open directly toward the **National School of Drama**, ensuring that students and performers are at their rehearsal halls within 120 seconds of exiting the train.

## **2. The Intellectual and Artistic Heartland**

Mandi House is the only neighborhood in Delhi where "Work-from-Home" often means rehearsing for a national play or researching at a world-class archive.

- **National School of Drama (NSD):** The Harvard of Indian Theater. Living nearby allows aspiring actors and technicians to be part of the late-night rehearsal culture that defines Mandi House.
