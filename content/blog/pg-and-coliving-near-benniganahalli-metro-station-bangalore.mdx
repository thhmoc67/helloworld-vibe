## **Why Benniganahalli is a Preferred Residential Hub**

Benniganahalli has emerged as a **preferred residential and professional hub** due to:

- **Metro connectivity:** Purple Line access to central Bangalore, MG Road, Whitefield, and Indiranagar
- **Proximity to IT corridors:** Close to EPIP Zone, Brigade Tech Park, and ITPL
- **Residential development:** Growing number of gated communities, apartments, PGs, and coliving properties

For professionals, living near Benniganahalli ensures **short commutes, modern amenities, and vibrant community living**.
