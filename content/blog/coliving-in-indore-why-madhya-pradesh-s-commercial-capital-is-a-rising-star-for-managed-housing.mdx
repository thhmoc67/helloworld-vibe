Indore occupies a unique position in India's urban landscape. Consistently ranked as India's cleanest city in central government surveys, it has earned a reputation for liveability that is genuinely deserved. It is also Madhya Pradesh's commercial and educational capital, home to IIM Indore, IIT Indore, a growing technology sector, and one of India's most dynamic small and medium enterprise ecosystems. For the young professionals and students drawn to these opportunities, managed coliving through HelloWorld is transforming the accommodation landscape.

## **Indore's Economic Profile: More Than Just a Clean City**

Indore's famous cleanliness is a point of civic pride, but the city's professional appeal goes well beyond its street sweeping. The city is the commercial heart of Madhya Pradesh, with a diversified economy spanning manufacturing, pharmaceuticals, textiles, food processing, financial services, and a growing IT and startup sector.

The Indore Special Economic Zone and various industrial areas host manufacturing and technology companies that employ thousands of professionals. The city's wholesale and retail trade ecosystem is one of the largest in central India. And the educational establishment  anchored by IIM Indore and IIT Indore, two of India's most prestigious institutions  attracts talented students from across the country and produces graduates who are in high demand nationally.

The combination of strong employment across multiple sectors, top-tier educational institutions, and a genuinely high quality of life makes Indore an increasingly attractive professional destination.

## **Best Neighbourhoods for Coliving in Indore**

**Vijay Nagar:** One of Indore's most developed and well-serviced residential areas, Vijay Nagar is a natural hub for coliving. It has excellent restaurants, shopping, entertainment infrastructure, and good connectivity to major employment zones.

**Scheme 54 and AB Road:** The primary commercial corridor of Indore, AB Road and its surrounding schemes host corporate offices, educational institutions, and residential developments. Well positioned for professionals working in Indore's commercial sector.

**Bhawarkua and Super Corridor:** The Super Corridor is Indore's answer to Bangalore's Outer Ring Road  a technology and commercial development corridor that is attracting IT companies, start-ups, and educational institutions. Properties in this area serve the professionals and students associated with this corridor.

**Rajwada and Old Indore:** For those who want to be in the heart of the city's historical and commercial core, areas near Rajwada offer central location with excellent access to Indore's famous street food and bazaar culture.

## **IIM Indore and IIT Indore: The Academic Ecosystem**

The presence of both IIM Indore and IIT Indore in the same city creates a uniquely dense academic and intellectual environment. These institutions attract faculty, researchers, administrative staff, and students who need quality managed accommodation.

For students at these institutions and at the many affiliated colleges and professional schools in the city, HelloWorld's managed accommodation provides a significant upgrade over the traditional student housing options available in Indore. The quality of facilities, the safety infrastructure, and the community environment are all materially better than what the unorganised student housing market typically offers.

## **Indore's Food Culture: A Genuine Highlight**

If there is one thing that virtually every professional or student who has spent time in Indore will mention, it is the food. Indore has one of India's most extraordinary street food cultures  a tradition of snacking and outdoor eating that has produced iconic dishes like poha-jalebi for breakfast, bhutte ka kees, garadu, and the legendary Sarafa Bazaar night food market, which operates through the early hours of the morning and serves as Indore's de facto social hub.

For HelloWorld residents, living in a city with this food culture is genuinely enriching. The social practice of exploring Indore's food scene  with fellow residents, with new colleagues, alone on an early morning walk  becomes one of the defining pleasures of life in the city.

## **The Cost Advantage of Indore**

Like Jaipur and Ahmedabad, Indore offers a significant cost-of-living advantage compared to India's primary tech metros. Housing, food, transport, and entertainment are all meaningfully more affordable than in Bangalore, Mumbai, or even Hyderabad.

For HelloWorld, this translates into managed coliving pricing that is accessible even for students and entry-level professionals on relatively modest incomes. The combination of quality accommodation, low cost, and high quality of life makes Indore one of India's most financially efficient professional destinations.
