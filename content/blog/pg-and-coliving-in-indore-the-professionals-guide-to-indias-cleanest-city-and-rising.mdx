## **2. Pithampur: The "Detroit of Central India"**

Located just on the outskirts of Indore, **Pithampur** is one of India’s largest industrial clusters. It is a critical hub for the automotive, pharmaceutical, and textile industries.

### **Industrial Giants in Pithampur:**

- **Automotive Sector:** Home to massive plants for **Eicher Motors**, **Mahindra & Mahindra**, **Force Motors**, and **VE Commercial Vehicles**.
- **Pharma Corridor:** Global pharmaceutical leaders like **Cipla**, **Lupin**, and **Glenmark** have their manufacturing and R&D bases here.
- **Manufacturing Hub:** Companies like **Case New Holland** and **Bridgestone** also have a significant presence.
