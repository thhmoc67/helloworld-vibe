Coimbatore’s industrial strength is legendary, housing over 25,000 small, medium, and large-scale industries.

### **Key Industrial Clusters:**

- **SIDCO Industrial Estate (Kurichi):** One of the oldest and most established zones, home to engineering, fabrication, and light manufacturing.
- **Malumichampatti & Eachanari:** The hub for heavy engineering and automotive giants. This area hosts massive facilities for **Larsen & Toubro (L&T)** and **Pricol**.
- **Mettupalayam Road & Thudiyalur:** Known for the **Pumps & Motors** industry. Companies like **Aquasub** and **Texmo** have turned Coimbatore into the "Pump City of Asia."
- **Aspen SEZ (Karumathampatti):** A specialized zone for high-value manufacturing, including the **L&T MBDA Missile Systems** facility.

## **2. The Rising Tech Hubs: Saravanampatti and Avinashi Road**

Coimbatore is Tamil Nadu’s second-largest IT hub. The city’s tech landscape is defined by sleek SEZs and modern corporate parks.

### **Major Business Landmarks:**

- **CHIL SEZ (Saravanampatti):** The nerve center of Coimbatore’s IT revolution. It hosts global giants like **Bosch, Cognizant, Amazon, and NTT Data**.
- **TIDEL Park & ELCOT SEZ:** Located near the airport and Avinashi Road, these parks house **TCS, Wipro, and HCL**.
- **KGISL Campus:** A massive private tech park that is home to several high-growth startups and international back-offices.
- **S.G. Highway (Avinashi Road):** The city’s main commercial artery, lined with premium office spaces, luxury hotels, and the best retail outlets like **Prozone Mall**.
