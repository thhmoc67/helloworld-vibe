Rithala is the western anchor of the Red Line, providing a unique "First-In, Last-Out" advantage for commuters.

## **2. Industrial & Commercial Hub: The Rithala-Wazirpur Belt**

Rithala is a critical node for Delhi’s small and medium-scale industries.

- **Rithala Industrial Area:** A specialized zone for plastic manufacturing, steel works, and logistics. It provides employment to thousands of technical professionals and engineers.
- **Warehouse Hub:** Due to its location on the city’s edge, Rithala has become a major warehousing spot for e-commerce giants. Professionals in supply chain management find Rithala the most convenient place to stay.
- **Proximity to Wazirpur:** A short drive or metro ride takes you to the **Wazirpur Industrial Area**, another massive cluster of manufacturing and trade.

Living at the end of the line doesn't mean you're at the end of the fun. Rithala is home to one of Delhi’s most iconic recreation spots.
