## **Neighborhood Overview: Kengeri Bus Terminal**

Kengeri Bus Terminal is part of **Bangalore’s southwestern corridor**, bordered by **Mysore Road**, one of the city’s main arterial roads connecting to the outskirts and satellite towns. Traditionally a **residential suburb**, the area has grown due to:

- **Improved metro and bus connectivity**
- **Affordable housing options compared to central Bangalore**
- **Proximity to colleges and schools along the Mysore Road corridor**

### **Residential Environment**

- **Apartments and gated communities**: Modern complexes with amenities like elevators, security, and community halls
- **Independent houses**: Affordable options for families and shared PG setups
- **Green spaces**: Parks and playgrounds for recreation

The calm suburban environment is particularly appealing to **students and young professionals** who prefer living away from the congestion of central Bangalore while still maintaining **easy access to the city**.

## **Connectivity and Commuting Advantages**

### **Metro Connectivity**

The **Kengeri Bus Terminal Metro Station** on the Purple Line connects residents to:

- **Central Bangalore**: Majestic, Cubbon Park, MG Road, and other key commercial areas
- **Western and suburban areas**: Nayandahalli, Mysore Road corridor
- **Last-mile commuting**: Auto-rickshaws, taxis, and BMTC buses for nearby neighborhoods

Unlike some other stations in the city, Kengeri Bus Terminal does **not have immediate interchanges** with other metro lines, but its **direct Purple Line access** ensures **smooth travel to central and western corridors**.

### **Road Access**

- **Mysore Road**: Primary arterial route connecting Kengeri to central Bangalore, electronic and industrial hubs, and the outskirts of the city
- **BMTC buses and private transport**: Frequent services connecting Kengeri to surrounding suburbs and schools/colleges

The combination of **metro and road connectivity** makes Kengeri ideal for **students, interns, and young professionals** who require **efficient commuting options**.
