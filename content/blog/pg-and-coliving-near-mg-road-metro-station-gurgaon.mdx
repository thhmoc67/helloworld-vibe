Running parallel to the NH-48 expressway through the heart of central Gurgaon, MG Road has been a commercial artery since Gurgaon's earliest phases of corporate development. The Metro station here sits at the centre of a mature urban environment with corporate offices, retail establishments, restaurants, and one of Gurgaon's most developed professional residential ecosystems.

For professionals who want to be within the established core of Gurgaon — walkable to commercial establishments, on the Metro for Delhi or Cyber City commutes, and surrounded by a mature professional community — accommodation near MG Road Metro station is among the city's best residential propositions.
