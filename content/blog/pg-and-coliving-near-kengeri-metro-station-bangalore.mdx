## **Connectivity and Commuting Advantages**

### **Metro Connectivity**

Kengeri Metro Station is the **last station on the Purple Line**, offering residents:

- Easy access to **central Bangalore hubs**, including Majestic, Cubbon Park, and MG Road
- Direct connection to **other southwestern suburban stations** along Mysore Road
- Efficient commuting for students, interns, and professionals without reliance on heavy traffic

Unlike other areas, Kengeri Metro Station primarily serves as a **residential terminus**, so it’s best suited for residents seeking **suburban living with convenient metro access**.

### **Road Connectivity**

- **Mysore Road**: A major arterial road linking Kengeri to central Bangalore and western suburbs
- **BMTC buses and taxis**: Provide last-mile connectivity within Kengeri and to nearby residential areas
- **Auto-rickshaws and app-based cabs**: Convenient for commuting within the neighborhood

This combination of **metro and road connectivity** ensures that residents can **reach educational institutions, commercial hubs, and central Bangalore efficiently**.
