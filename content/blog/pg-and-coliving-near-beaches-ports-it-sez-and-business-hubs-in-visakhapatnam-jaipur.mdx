Visakhapatnam, Jaipur, Kolkata, and Chennai each have distinctive professional profiles and housing needs that differ meaningfully from the standard technology-corridor accommodation model of Bangalore or Hyderabad. ## **Visakhapatnam: Coliving Near Beaches, Ports, and IT SEZ**

Visakhapatnam — Vizag — is Andhra Pradesh's most important port city and one of south India's most beautiful urban environments. The city's economy combines heavy industry, defence, port operations, and a growing technology sector with a natural setting of extraordinary quality.

**IT SEZ and Madhurawada technology corridor:** Vizag's primary technology employment zone, home to Cognizant, Tech Mahindra, Infosys, and other IT companies. Professionals working in this corridor prioritise accommodation in Madhurawada, Rushikonda, and MVP Colony — areas that combine proximity to the IT SEZ with good beach access.

Key search terms: PG near IT SEZ Visakhapatnam, coliving near Madhurawada, managed accommodation near Rushikonda beach, PG near tech park Vizag, coliving near IT park Visakhapatnam.

**Port and industrial zones:** Vizag's port and steel plant are major employment centres. Professionals working in these industries need accommodation in Gajuwaka, Seethammadhara, and surrounding areas.

Key search terms: PG near Vizag port, coliving near steel plant Visakhapatnam, managed accommodation Gajuwaka.

**Beach proximity as a quality-of-life criterion:** Unlike most Indian cities where natural environment is distant from residential areas, Vizag's beaches — Rushikonda, Rishikonda, RK Beach — are within the residential zone of the city. For HelloWorld residents in beach-adjacent properties, the beach is not a weekend destination but a daily commute to work alternative.

## **Jaipur: Coliving Near Tourist Attractions, Markets, Colleges, and Business Hubs**

Jaipur's professional geography is more diverse than its image as a tourist city might suggest. The city has a growing technology sector, strong manufacturing and gems industry, excellent educational institutions, and significant hospitality and tourism industry employment.

**Malviya Nagar and IT corridors:** The primary technology employment zone for Jaipur's IT and ITES sector. Companies including Infosys, Wipro, and numerous IT service firms have Jaipur operations in this corridor.

Key search terms: PG near Malviya Nagar Jaipur, coliving near IT park Jaipur, managed accommodation near Sitapura Jaipur.

**C-Scheme and central business district:** Jaipur's commercial heart, home to corporate offices, insurance companies, banks, and government institutions. Well-connected residential areas serve professionals working in this zone.

Key search terms: PG near C-Scheme Jaipur, coliving near central Jaipur, managed accommodation near MI Road.

**Tourism and hospitality industry proximity:** Jaipur's tourism industry is one of India's most significant. The major heritage sites Amber Fort, City Palace, Hawa Mahal  are employment centres for the hospitality, guiding, and tourism services industries. Professionals working in this sector value accommodation with good connectivity to the heritage zone.

Key search terms: PG near Amber Fort area Jaipur, coliving near tourist zone Jaipur, managed accommodation near heritage area.

**University and college areas:** BITS Pilani Jaipur, Malaviya NIT, Rajasthan University, and dozens of other institutions generate student accommodation demand. Areas near these institutions — Jawahar Nagar, Malviya Nagar, and the university areas — have strong student housing demand.

Key search terms: student PG near BITS Pilani Jaipur, coliving near NIT Jaipur, managed accommodation near university Jaipur.

## **Kolkata: Coliving Near Metro, Salt Lake IT Hub, and Cultural Areas**

Kolkata's housing market is shaped by two primary factors: the Metro-Railway network and the geography of the city's technology sector in Salt Lake and New Town.

**Salt Lake Sector V Metro connectivity:** The Metro's connection to Salt Lake Sector V has transformed commuting options for technology professionals. Stations at Salt Lake Sector V, Salt Lake Stadium, and City Centre provide direct Metro connectivity from residential areas across Kolkata.

Key search terms: PG near Salt Lake Sector V Metro Kolkata, coliving near IT hub Kolkata, managed accommodation near Sector V, PG near New Town Kolkata, coliving near Rajarhat.

**Railway junction proximity:** Kolkata's suburban railway network complements the Metro. Professionals living near Sealdah, Howrah, or suburban railway stations have access to the broader rail network for both city commuting and intercity travel.

Key search terms: PG near Sealdah station Kolkata, coliving near Howrah, managed accommodation near railway station Kolkata.

**Cultural hubs:** Kolkata's cultural geography — College Street, Park Street, Rabindra Sarani, Gariahat — shapes the residential preferences of the city's creative and academic professionals. Proximity to these cultural zones is a positive lifestyle factor for many residents.

Key search terms: PG near Park Street Kolkata, coliving near College Street, managed accommodation near cultural hub Kolkata.

## **Chennai: Coliving Near OMR IT Highway, Metro, and Cultural Hubs**

Chennai's professional geography is anchored by the OMR (Old Mahabalipuram Road) IT Highway and the city's suburban railway network.

**OMR corridor:** Chennai's primary technology employment zone stretches 20+ kilometres from Perungudi to Siruseri. Companies including TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Cognizant, and hundreds of others have OMR campuses.

Key search terms: PG near OMR Chennai, coliving near IT Highway Chennai, managed accommodation near Sholinganallur, PG near Perungudi, coliving near Siruseri IT park Chennai.

**Chennai Metro connectivity:** Chennai's Phase 1 Metro (Blue and Green lines) and the expanding Phase 2 network are increasingly important for commuting. Metro-adjacent coliving offers professionals a traffic-free commuting alternative.

Key search terms: coliving near Metro station Chennai, PG near Blue Line Chennai, managed accommodation near Green Line, coliving near Koyambedu Metro.

**MRTS (Mass Rapid Transit System):** Chennai's MRTS rail line runs along the city's eastern coast, connecting Beach station to Velachery and serving the IT professionals in the OMR-adjacent residential areas.

Key search terms: PG near MRTS station Chennai, coliving near Velachery, managed accommodation near Thiruvanmiyur.

**Cultural and heritage areas:** Chennai's Mylapore, Besant Nagar, and Adyar areas have strong cultural identities alongside professional residential character. Proximity to Marina Beach, the Mylapore temple complex, and the Besant Nagar café scene adds lifestyle value to accommodation in these areas.

Key search terms: PG near Besant Nagar Chennai, coliving near Mylapore, managed accommodation near Marina Beach Chennai, PG near Adyar.
