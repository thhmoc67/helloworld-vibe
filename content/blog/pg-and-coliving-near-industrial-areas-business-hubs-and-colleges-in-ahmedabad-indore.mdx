## **Ahmedabad: Coliving Near GIFT City, SG Road, and Industrial Areas**

Ahmedabad's professional geography has several distinct zones. Understanding which zone is most relevant to your work or study destination is the first step in finding the right accommodation.

**GIFT City (Gujarat International Finance Tec-City):** India's first operational international financial services city, located between Ahmedabad and Gandhinagar. Home to offices of major financial institutions, fintech companies, and global service providers. For professionals working in GIFT City, accommodation in Gandhinagar, Motera, or northern Ahmedabad provides the most practical commute.

**SG Road (Sarkhej-Gandhinagar Highway):** Ahmedabad's primary commercial corridor, lined with corporate offices, IT companies, banks, and commercial establishments. Residential areas in Prahlad Nagar, Satellite, Vastrapur, and Bodakdev serve SG Road professionals.

**GIDC (Gujarat Industrial Development Corporation) areas:** Multiple GIDC industrial zones across Ahmedabad and surrounding districts host manufacturing companies in textiles, pharmaceuticals, chemicals, and engineering. Professionals working in these zones need accommodation in nearby residential areas.

**Educational hubs:** IIM Ahmedabad, NIRMA University, Gujarat University, and dozens of other institutions generate student accommodation demand across the city.

## **Indore: Coliving Near Super Corridor, AB Road, and IIM Indore**

Indore's professional geography is anchored by the Super Corridor — the city's technology and commercial development spine — and by the presence of two of India's most prestigious educational institutions.

**Super Corridor:** Indore's primary technology and commercial development zone, attracting IT companies, startups, and educational institutions. Residential areas along and adjacent to the Super Corridor serve the professionals and students working and studying in this zone.

**AB Road (Agra-Bombay Road):** Indore's commercial spine, lined with offices, educational institutions, hospitals, and commercial establishments. Well-connected residential areas serve the professional population working along this corridor.

**IIM Indore campus area:** One of India's premier management institutions, IIM Indore's campus attracts faculty, staff, students, and associated professionals who need quality accommodation in the vicinity.

**IIT Indore area:** India's prestigious engineering institution generates accommodation demand among students, faculty, and research staff.

**Vijay Nagar and surrounding areas:** Indore's most developed residential zone, offering excellent social infrastructure and connectivity to major employment and educational corridors.

## **Coimbatore: Coliving Near IT Parks, Textile Mills, and Colleges**
