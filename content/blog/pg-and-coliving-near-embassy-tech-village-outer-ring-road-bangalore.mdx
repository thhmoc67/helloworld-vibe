## **Understanding the ORR Corridor: Bangalore's Spine**

The Outer Ring Road runs in a broad arc from Hebbal in the north to Electronic City in the south, passing through the eastern and south-eastern parts of Bangalore. This 60-kilometre ring road has become Bangalore's most important employment corridor, hosting office parks, standalone corporate campuses, and technology companies at virtually every point along its length.

Embassy Tech Village sits in the ORR's central-eastern section, near the junction of the ORR and the Sarjapur Road. This location makes it one of the most accessible technology parks on the entire ORR — with residential areas including Bellandur, Kadubeesanahalli, HSR Layout, Marathahalli, and Sarjapur Road all within reasonable commuting distance.
