## **Metro Connectivity – The Biggest Advantage**

Konanakunte Cross is on the **Green Line**, which connects **North and South Bangalore seamlessly**.

### **Direct Metro Access**

- Northbound:  
**Yelachenahalli → Jayanagar → Lalbagh → Majestic → Yeshwanthpur → Nagasandra**
- Southbound:  
**Doddakallasandra → Vajrahalli → Silk Institute**

### **Yellow Line Interchange**

At **RV Road**, you can switch to the **Yellow Line**, giving access to:

- Electronic City
- Silk Board
- HSR Layout

This is a **huge advantage for IT professionals**.
