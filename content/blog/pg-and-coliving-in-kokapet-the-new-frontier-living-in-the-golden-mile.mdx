### **2. Transit & Connectivity: The Signal-Free Life**

Infrastructure in Kokapet isn't just about roads; it's about smart connectivity that bypasses the traditional city grind.

- **The "Golden Mile" Road Network:** Neopolis features HMDA-designed **100-foot and 120-foot wide roads**, ensuring that even at peak hours, the movement within the district remains fluid and signal-free.
- **Direct ORR Access:** Kokapet sits right on the **Outer Ring Road (ORR)**. This allows professionals to reach **Rajiv Gandhi International Airport** in under 25 minutes and provides a direct, high-speed link to **Narsingi, Tellapur, and Patancheru**.
- **The Trump Tower Influence:** The arrival of global luxury brands like Trump Tower has pushed the local infrastructure to international standards, including advanced stormwater management and dedicated cycling tracks.
- **Proximity to the Airport Metro:** The upcoming **Airport Express Metro** will feature accessible points near the Kokapet-Financial District junction, further reducing the reliance on private vehicles for long-distance city travel.
