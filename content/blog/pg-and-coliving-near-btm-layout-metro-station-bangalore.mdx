Positioned between **Jayadeva Hospital** and **Central Silk Board**, this station serves a dense and highly active urban zone.

BTM Layout has long been known as a **startup hub, student hub, and budget-friendly residential area**, and with the metro becoming operational, it is transforming into an even more **powerful coliving and PG hotspot**.

If you are searching for:

- PG near BTM Layout Metro Station
- Coliving near Silk Board
- Affordable housing near Electronic City corridor

Then BTM Layout is one of the **most practical and high-value locations in Bangalore**.

BTM Layout is not just popular—it is **one of the most consistently demanded rental micro-markets**.

## **Metro Connectivity – Game Changer for BTM**

BTM Layout Metro Station lies on the **Yellow Line**, which directly connects **South Bangalore to Electronic City**.

### **Direct Metro Access**

- RV Road (interchange with Green Line)
- Ragigudda
- Jayadeva Hospital (interchange with Pink Line)
- Central Silk Board
- Bommanahalli
- Electronic City
- Bommasandra

### **Why This Matters**

Earlier, BTM suffered due to **Silk Board traffic congestion**. With metro:

- Travel time reduces drastically
- Dependence on buses/cabs decreases
- Office commute becomes predictable

This makes BTM even more attractive for:

Working professionals and daily commuters
