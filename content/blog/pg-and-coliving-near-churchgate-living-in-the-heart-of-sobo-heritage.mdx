Infrastructure in South Mumbai has seen its biggest upgrade in decades, making the "commute" a relic of the past.

- **The Coastal Road Phase 2:** As of mid-2026, the Coastal Road has slashed travel times between South Mumbai and the Western Suburbs by 70%, making a weekend trip to Bandra or Juhu a breezy 20-minute drive.
- **Western Railway Terminus:** Churchgate remains the starting point for the Western Line, providing a "seat guarantee" for those traveling north to Dadar, Andheri, or Borivali.
- **The Cuffe Parade Extension:** The Metro's extension to Cuffe Parade has seamlessly integrated the southern-most residential tip with the Churchgate transit hub.
