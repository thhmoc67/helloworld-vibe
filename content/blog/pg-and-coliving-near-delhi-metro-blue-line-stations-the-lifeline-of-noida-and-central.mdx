### **Noida Sector 52: The Major Interchange**

Sector 52 is one of the most strategic stops on the Blue Line. It features a dedicated interchange to the **Aqua Line (Noida Sector 51)** via a skywalk.

- **Why stay here?** If you work in Greater Noida (via Aqua Line) but want to be close to the social life and connectivity of the Blue Line, Sector 52/34 is the sweet spot.
- **Lifestyle:** You are minutes away from the buzzing markets of Sector 18 and the corporate parks of Sector 62.

The final stop before the Metro crosses the Hindon River into Delhi, **Sector 15** is a favorite for those who have one foot in Delhi and the other in Noida.
