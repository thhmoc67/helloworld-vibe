## **1. Hinjewadi: Rajiv Gandhi Infotech Park**

Often called the "Silicon Valley of Pune," this is India's second-largest IT park. Spread across 2,800 acres and divided into Phase 1, 2, and 3, it is home to over 800 companies.

### **Major Inhabitants:**

- **Phase 1:Wipro, Infosys, Cognizant, and Tata Technologies**. This area is the most commercially developed with malls and high-end dining.
- **Phase 2:Accenture, Tech Mahindra, IBM, and Credit Suisse**. It’s the densest part of the park in terms of office buildings.
- **Phase 3:TCS (Sahyadri Park), KPIT, and LTIMindtree**. Known for being greener and more spacious.
