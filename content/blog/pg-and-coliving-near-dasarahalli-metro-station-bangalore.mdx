Dasarahalli is a **suburban area along the northwestern corridor**, bordered by **Nagasandra and Jalahalli**, with a mix of **residential complexes, industrial units, and educational institutions**. ### **Residential Environment**

- **Gated communities and apartment complexes**: Providing modern amenities, security, and recreation areas
- **Independent houses and villas**: Common in older neighborhoods; often converted into **PG or shared housing**
- **Industrial and semi-industrial areas**: Offering local employment opportunities, influencing the need for nearby accommodations

Dasarahalli offers a **balance between quiet suburban living and easy access to Bangalore’s metro network**, making it suitable for **long-term residents, students, and professionals alike**.
