## **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)**

**1. Is the Aqua Line connected to the Delhi Metro?**

Yes. You can interchange at Noida Sector 51 (Aqua Line) to Noida Sector 52 (Blue Line). A new 420-meter air-conditioned skywalk with travelators makes this transfer quick and comfortable.

**2. Can I use my Delhi Metro Smart Card on the Aqua Line?**

No, the Aqua Line uses its own ticketing system. You can buy a QR-code paper ticket, use the NMRC "SBI City 1" card, or use the NMRC mobile app.
