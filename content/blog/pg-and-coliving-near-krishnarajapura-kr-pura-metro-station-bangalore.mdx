## **Understanding KR Pura Area**

KR Pura is strategically located at the junction of **Whitefield Road, Old Madras Road, and Varthur Road**, connecting eastern Bangalore to central and southern parts of the city.

### **Key features of KR Pura:**

- **Proximity to Whitefield IT corridor:** ITPL, Brigade Tech Park, and other corporate offices within 10–15 minutes
- **Residential growth:** Increasing number of gated communities, apartment complexes, PGs, and coliving spaces
- **Metro access:** KR Pura Metro Station provides **direct connectivity to MG Road, Indiranagar, and Whitefield**

This combination of transit connectivity, IT hub access, and modern housing makes KR Pura a **high-demand location for professionals**.
