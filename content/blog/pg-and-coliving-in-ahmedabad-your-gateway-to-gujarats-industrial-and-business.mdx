Ahmedabad is the heart of India's manufacturing sector. If you are an engineer or a supply chain specialist, you are likely working in one of these massive industrial zones.

### **Key Industrial Clusters:**

- **Sanand (The Auto Hub):** Famous for hosting **Tata Motors (Nano & EV plants)**, **Ford**, and **Micron Technology’s** new semiconductor facility. Sanand is a global manufacturing landmark.
- **Changodar:** A massive hub for **Pharmaceuticals** and **Logistics**, housing giants like **Zydus Lifesciences**, **Intas Pharmaceuticals**, and **Aurobindo Pharma**.
- **Naroda & Vatva:** The traditional industrial soul of Ahmedabad, home to thousands of chemical, textile, and engineering firms.

## **2. Modern Business Hubs: GIFT City, S.G. Highway, and Prahlad Nagar**

For those in Finance, IT, and Tech, Ahmedabad’s skyline is dominated by ultra-modern glass towers and specialized business districts.

### **Major Business Districts:**

- **GIFT City (Gujarat International Finance Tec-City):** India’s first operational smart city and a global financial hub. It hosts **Google, Oracle, IBM, HSBC**, and the **National Stock Exchange (NSE)**. It is the future of Indian fintech.
- **S.G. Highway (Sarkhej-Gandhinagar Highway):** The city’s main commercial artery. It is lined with corporate parks like **Titanium Square** and **Mondeal Heights**, housing firms like **Adani Group**, **PwC**, and **KPMG**.
- **Prahlad Nagar:** A posh business hub known for its corporate parks and vibrant café culture. It’s a favorite for startups and digital marketing agencies.
