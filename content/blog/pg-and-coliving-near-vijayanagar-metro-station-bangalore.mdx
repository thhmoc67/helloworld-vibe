## **Connectivity and Commuting Benefits**

Vijayanagar Metro Station’s position on the Purple Line provides **direct connectivity to major employment hubs, central business districts, and other metro lines**. Commuting from Vijayanagar is efficient and predictable, making it ideal for professionals who need to navigate the city daily.

The station also allows **easy interchange at Majestic (Nadaprabhu Kempegowda Station)**, connecting commuters to the Green Line, which covers eastern parts of Bangalore, including Whitefield and Indiranagar. Road access via Mysore Road, Tumkur Road, and surrounding arterial roads further enhances mobility.

Public transport options, including **BMTC buses, auto-rickshaws, and ride-sharing services**, complement metro connectivity, ensuring **seamless last-mile access** for residents.
