## **Why Majestic is a Prime Residential and Transit Hub**

Majestic’s importance stems from several factors:

- **Strategic Location:** Connects Purple Line (east-west) and Green Line (north-south), providing **metro access to Whitefield, Challaghatta, Nagasandra, and Yelachenahalli**
- **Central Access:** Adjacent to Majestic bus station, making intercity and local commuting seamless
- **Connectivity to IT and corporate corridors:** Indiranagar, Whitefield, MG Road, and ITPL accessible via metro or road

For professionals and students seeking **convenient urban living**, Majestic is an ideal location.
