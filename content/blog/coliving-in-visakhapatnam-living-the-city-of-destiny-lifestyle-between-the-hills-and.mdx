## **2. The Port & Industrial Core: Gajuwaka and Scindia**

South Vizag is where the city’s industrial "muscle" resides. It is home to the **Visakhapatnam Port Authority (VPA)**—one of India’s largest and busiest ports—and a massive PSU cluster.

### **Industrial & PSU Giants:**

- **Manufacturing & Steel:** The **Visakhapatnam Steel Plant (RINL)** and **Hindustan Shipyard** are legendary institutions employing generations of engineers.
- **Energy & Oil:HPCL**, **IOCL**, and **BPCL** maintain massive refineries and corporate offices in the **Malkapuram and Scindia** areas.
- **Public Sector Hubs:BHEL (HPVP)** and **NTPC Simhadri** are key players in the city’s energy and manufacturing landscape.
