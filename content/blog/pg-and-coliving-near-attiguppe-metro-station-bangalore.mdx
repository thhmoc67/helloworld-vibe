## **Connectivity and Commuting Advantages**

Attiguppe Metro Station’s location on the Purple Line offers **direct and reliable connectivity to key employment, educational, and commercial hubs**.

- **Purple Line Access**: Connects commuters to Kengeri, Mysore Road, Nayandahalli, Vijayanagar, and Majestic for interchanges to the Green Line.
- **Road Network**: Adjacent to Mysore Road and arterial city roads, allowing access to Rajajinagar, Rajarajeshwari Nagar, and western IT corridors.
- **Public Transport**: BMTC buses, auto-rickshaws, and ride-sharing services provide last-mile connectivity.

Living near Attiguppe Metro **significantly reduces commuting time**, particularly for IT professionals, interns, and students traveling to western Bangalore and central business districts.
