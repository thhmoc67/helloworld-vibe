## **Whitefield in Bangalore’s Employment Geography**

Whitefield is situated in east Bangalore along the **Old Airport Road and Varthur Road corridors**, with the Whitefield Metro Station on the Purple Line providing direct connectivity to central Bangalore. Unlike central zones such as MG Road, Whitefield primarily serves as an **employment and IT residential hub**, and its development over the last two decades reflects this focus.

Key employment clusters in Whitefield include:

- **ITPL (International Tech Park Bangalore):** One of Bangalore’s largest IT parks, hosting global companies such as Accenture, Cisco, Oracle, and many mid-sized software firms.
- **EPIP Zone and surrounding office parks:** A cluster of mid-sized IT companies, service providers, and startups.
- **Whitefield IT corridors along Varthur Road:** Rapidly developing professional neighborhoods, including business parks, co-working spaces, and tech offices.

The Whitefield Metro Station (Kadugodi) serves as the principal access point for commuting professionals, connecting the area directly to **Indiranagar, MG Road, Baiyappanahalli, and central Bangalore** via the Purple Line.

## **Micro-Markets Around Whitefield for Accommodation**

Understanding the residential micro-markets is critical for selecting the right accommodation near Whitefield Metro Station.

### **Kadugodi**

Kadugodi is immediately adjacent to the metro station and is one of the most practical residential zones for professionals working in Whitefield. It offers a mix of independent apartments, PG accommodations, and coliving spaces. Its proximity ensures minimal commuting time.

### **Whitefield Main Road and EPIP Zone**

These areas include apartments and managed coliving spaces within short commuting distance to ITPL and surrounding offices. While slightly more expensive than Kadugodi, they offer better access to social infrastructure, including malls, restaurants, and gyms.

### **Varthur Road Stretch**

The Varthur corridor is popular among mid-level and senior professionals who prefer slightly larger accommodations. It is well connected to Whitefield Metro and IT parks but may involve a longer walk or short feeder ride from the station.

### **Sarjapur Road Linkages**

Though Sarjapur Road is further away, some professionals prefer properties along feeder routes connecting to Whitefield Metro for a balance between affordability and connectivity.
