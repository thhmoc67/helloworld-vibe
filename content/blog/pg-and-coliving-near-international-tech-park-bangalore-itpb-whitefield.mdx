## **The Whitefield Context: Understanding the Eastern Corridor**

Whitefield occupies a unique position in Bangalore's geography. Located approximately 18 to 22 kilometres east of the city centre, it was until the late 1990s a relatively quiet suburb known primarily as an Anglo-Indian settlement. The establishment of ITPB and subsequent development of other technology parks in the area transformed Whitefield into one of Bangalore's most important employment zones.

Today, the broader Whitefield corridor — encompassing ITPB, the Prestige Tech Park, Salarpuria Softzone, and numerous standalone office campuses — employs several hundred thousand technology professionals. This enormous employment concentration has driven the development of a complete residential and social ecosystem around the original technology park.

The Namma Metro Purple Line's eastern extension to Whitefield has dramatically improved connectivity from this previously Metro-isolated corridor. The extension, with stations at Whitefield, Nallurhalli, Pattandur Agrahara, Kadugodi, and others, connects ITPB professionals directly to central Bangalore via the Purple Line.
